Our Story

The UNKNOWN Origin

From the ancient ghats of the Ganges to the cutting-edge laboratories of Jaipur — a journey of rediscovery.

UNKNOWN was born from a simple, powerful question: What if the skincare secrets of ancient India were unlocked with the precision of modern science?

Our founder, Dr. Priya Mehta, spent seven years traveling across India — from the saffron fields of Pampore to the Gir cow sanctuaries of Gujarat — documenting the traditional preparation methods that have been passed down through generations of Ayurvedic families.

What she discovered was extraordinary. These weren't just folk remedies. They were sophisticated bioactive formulations, refined over millennia, that modern science is only now beginning to understand.

The Bilona Revelation

The turning point came in a small village near Porbandar, where Dr. Mehta witnessed the Bilona method firsthand. The difference between machine-processed ghee and hand-churned Bilona ghee was not just qualitative — it was molecular. The traditional method preserved a complex matrix of fatty acids, vitamins, and bioactive compounds that industrial processing destroyed.

This discovery became the foundation of UNKNOWN. We committed to sourcing only Bilona ghee, made by the same families who have been making it for centuries. And we committed to applying rigorous scientific validation to every traditional claim.

The Laboratory of Tradition

Today, UNKNOWN operates a unique hybrid facility in Jaipur. On one side, Vaidyas prepare traditional decoctions and infusions using methods unchanged for thousands of years. On the other, biochemists analyze, stabilize, and enhance these preparations using state-of-the-art extraction and encapsulation technologies.

The result is skincare that honors its origins while delivering results that meet the highest standards of modern dermatology. Every UNKNOWN product carries both the wisdom of the past and the promise of the future.

Ingredients

Full transparency on every element that touches your skin.

A2 Bilona Ghee

Ghrita — The Elixir of Life

Sourced from indigenous Gir cows in Gujarat, our ghee is produced using the ancient Bilona method: curd is hand-churned in a wooden churner, and the butter is slow-cooked over a wood fire. This 48-hour process yields ghee with a unique fatty acid profile rich in butyric acid, conjugated linoleic acid (CLA), and vitamins A, D, E, and K2.

Scientific Benefits

  • Butyric acid repairs and strengthens the skin barrier
  • Vitamin K2 improves skin elasticity and reduces wrinkles
  • CLA provides anti-inflammatory and antioxidant protection
  • Deeply penetrates the dermis for cellular nourishment

Sourcing

Partner cooperative in Junagadh, Gujarat. Cows are free-grazed on organic pasture. Zero antibiotics or hormones.

Prickly Pear Cactus Seed Oil

Nagfani — Desert's Liquid Gold

Harvested from the Thar Desert, each liter of this precious oil requires approximately 30 kg of seeds — about one million seeds. Cold-pressed to preserve its extraordinary nutrient density, it contains the highest vitamin E concentration of any plant oil, plus rare betalains and exceptional levels of linoleic acid.

Scientific Benefits

  • Vitamin E content 150% higher than argan oil
  • Betalains provide superior antioxidant protection
  • Linoleic acid restores skin's lipid barrier
  • Reduces hyperpigmentation and dark spots

Sourcing

Wild-harvested by desert communities in Rajasthan. Fair-trade certified. Seeds are a byproduct of fruit processing, ensuring zero waste.

Kumkumadi Oil

Kumkumadi Tailam — The Complexion Elixir

A classical Ayurvedic formulation documented in the Bhaishajya Ratnavali, containing saffron, sandalwood, turmeric, licorice, and 17 other precious herbs. Each ingredient is processed individually before being combined in a specific sequence and cooked in sesame oil over a slow flame for 72 hours.

Scientific Benefits

  • Saffron crocin inhibits melanin overproduction
  • Sandalwood alpha-santalol reduces inflammation
  • Licorice glabridin brightens and evens skin tone
  • Sesame lignans provide deep antioxidant protection

Sourcing

Prepared by third-generation Vaidya family in Jaipur. All herbs are organic and sustainably wild-crafted or cultivated.

Kashmiri Saffron

Kesar — The Red Gold

Grade A1 Mongra saffron from Pampore, Kashmir — the finest grade available. Each strand is hand-picked from the Crocus sativus flower during the brief autumn blooming season. Our saffron contains crocin levels exceeding 250, the highest standard for therapeutic grade.

Scientific Benefits

  • Crocin improves skin texture and radiance
  • Safranal provides mood-enhancing aromatherapy
  • Kaempferol offers powerful antioxidant activity
  • Promotes collagen synthesis and cell renewal

Sourcing

Direct partnership with Pampore farming cooperative. ISO-certified grading. Harvested within 24 hours of blooming.

Sustainability

Luxury without compromise — for your skin and the earth.

Packaging Philosophy

We believe the most beautiful packaging is the kind that disappears. Every UNKNOWN product is housed in amber glass — infinitely recyclable and UV-protective. Our outer boxes are made from seed-embedded paper that, when planted, grows into wildflowers. Even our shipping materials are compostable cornstarch foam and recycled paper.

We have eliminated all single-use plastics from our supply chain. Our commitment: by 2026, every element of our packaging will be either reusable, recyclable, or compostable.

Ethical Sourcing

Our ingredient network spans the length and breadth of India, and every partnership is built on principles of fairness, transparency, and mutual respect. We pay 30% above market rates to our farming cooperatives. We provide technical training and organic certification support. And we commit to long-term contracts that give our partners the security to invest in sustainable practices.

Our A2 Bilona Ghee comes from a cooperative of 120 Gir cow herders in Gujarat. Our Prickly Pear seeds are collected by desert communities in Rajasthan who have been harvesting cactus fruit for generations. Our saffron is sourced from a women's cooperative in Pampore, Kashmir, where the tradition of saffron cultivation has been passed from mother to daughter for centuries.

Carbon Neutrality

UNKNOWN is committed to achieving net-zero carbon emissions by 2027. We have already offset 100% of our shipping emissions through verified reforestation projects in the Western Ghats. Our Jaipur facility runs on 80% solar power. And for every order placed, we plant a tree through our partnership with Grow-Trees.com.

To date, our community has planted over 12,000 trees — a small forest that will sequester approximately 240 tonnes of CO2 over its lifetime.

FAQs

Everything you need to know about UNKNOWN.

What makes UNKNOWN different from other luxury skincare brands?
UNKNOWN is the only luxury brand that combines authentic Ayurvedic preparation methods — like the Bilona hand-churning technique — with modern biotechnology. Our ingredients are sourced directly from farming cooperatives across India, and every formula is clinically tested for efficacy while honoring traditional wisdom.
Are UNKNOWN products suitable for sensitive skin?
Yes. All UNKNOWN products are dermatologist-tested and formulated without common irritants like synthetic fragrances, sulfates, parabens, or artificial colors. Our ingredients are 100% natural and we provide full ingredient transparency for every product. However, we recommend patch testing before full use.
How long will it take to see results?
Most customers notice improved hydration and radiance within the first week. For more significant concerns like pigmentation, fine lines, or texture issues, we recommend consistent use for 6–8 weeks. Ayurvedic skincare works at the cellular level, and true transformation takes time.
What is your return policy?
We offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee. If you are not completely happy with your purchase, contact us for a full refund or exchange. We want you to love your UNKNOWN experience.
Do you offer international shipping?
Yes, we ship worldwide. Orders within India arrive in 3–5 business days. International orders typically arrive in 7–14 business days. All shipping is carbon-neutral through our partnership with verified reforestation projects.
Can I subscribe to regular deliveries?
Absolutely. Our subscription service delivers your favorite products every 30, 60, or 90 days with a 15% discount and free shipping. You can pause, modify, or cancel anytime.
Are your products vegan?
Some of our products contain ethically sourced beeswax and A2 Bilona Ghee (clarified butter from cows). These are not vegan. However, we clearly label all products, and our Gentle Cleanser and certain serums are fully vegan. We are working on expanding our vegan range.
What is the shelf life of UNKNOWN products?
Because we use natural preservatives and avoid synthetic stabilizers, our products have a shelf life of 12 months unopened and 6 months after opening. We recommend storing them in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight.
